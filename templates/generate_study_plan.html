Tips for Effective Studying

  • Consistency is key: Regular, scheduled study sessions are more effective than cramming
  • Active recall: Test yourself on the material rather than just re-reading it
  • Spaced repetition: Review material at increasing intervals to improve long-term retention
  • Take breaks: Short breaks during study sessions help maintain focus and improve learning
  • Varied practice: Mix up different topics and problem types in each study session
